🗺️ How It Works · For Venues

Verified foot traffic.
Zero marketing spend.

The Board-Bored trail turns First Friday attendees into guaranteed visitors at your door.

1

Support & Join

Contribute to the trail as a supporter or donate a Prize of the Month. You're listed in the vendor hub and in the app for the night.

2

Display Your QR

We give you a live rotating QR code for the evening. It's anti-screenshot, expires every 60 seconds — only scannable in-person.

3

Attendees Scan & Visit

App users get their stamp when they physically scan your code. That's a verified visit — not a click, not an impression.

4

See Your Data

After the event, your verified scan count is published here. Compare with past events. See your growth month over month.

5

Winner Drawn at 9 PM

Attendees who complete all 5 stamps get an entry code. One winner receives the Prize of the Month — a donated item with a story, announced live at ACK.

6

Repeat Monthly

First Friday is every first Friday, October–June. Your cumulative data builds here — proof that the trail keeps delivering.
